Sensor Fusion · ROS2

SenseNav — Multi-Sensor Localization

Integrated GPS, IMU (VectorNav VN-100), and camera in ROS2 Jazzy. Implemented Extended Kalman Filter with GPS–VO frame alignment via SVD. Reduced monocular VO drift from 45.8% → 3.1% using IMU-aided approach. Tested across indoor, outdoor, and transition scenarios (444s total data).

3R Planar Arm — Forward Dynamics

Simulated a 3-link planar arm falling under gravity. Derived full dynamics — mass matrix from Jacobians, gravity torques via virtual work, Coriolis terms via Christoffel symbols. RK4 integration over 30,000 timesteps achieved 0.0002% energy conservation error.

Legged Robotics · Mechanical Design

The Ostrich — Bipedal Robot

Designed a 10-DOF bipedal robot leg mechanism using the SLIP model framework. Full SolidWorks design, inverse kinematics solver (12 equations), 10 stepper motors with TB6600 drivers and Teensy 4.1. Physical prototype via hybrid CNC and 3D printing. Government funded through SSIP.
